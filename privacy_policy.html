Privacy Policy for the "Technological Usage Patterns Among Students" Study

1. Acceptance of Terms

This Privacy Policy is an integral part of your participation in the "Technological Usage Patterns Among Students" study. By accessing and using our survey platform, you signify your explicit understanding and acceptance of the terms outlined here. This policy applies to all data collected during your participation in the study. If at any time you find yourself in disagreement with any part of this policy, we respectfully request that you abstain from participating in the study. Your participation is entirely voluntary and is subject to the terms presented herein.

2. Information Collection

We engage in a comprehensive data collection process to enhance the validity and accuracy of our research findings. This process includes the collection of:

  • Detailed responses to survey questions, which may include personal opinions, experiences, and demographic information.
  • Specific device information, such as the type of device (mobile, tablet, desktop), operating system, browser type, and other related technical details to understand the technology usage patterns.
  • Interaction metrics with our survey and externally hosted pages, including the time spent, scrolling behavior, and any interactions with the content, to analyze engagement levels.
  • Timestamps and frequency of interactions with our survey platform to monitor participation rates and study behavioral patterns over time.

3. Use of Information

The collected information serves multiple research purposes:

  • To gain a comprehensive understanding of the technological habits and preferences among university students.
  • To perform in-depth analyses of how different demographic groups interact with technology.
  • To correlate technology usage patterns with academic and personal outcomes.
  • The information will help in understanding the general attitudes towards privacy policies and data protection awareness among the student population.

4. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your data. Here are our rigorous measures:

  • All data is securely stored on encrypted servers managed by Utrecht University and MB Web Solutions.
  • Regular audits and updates are conducted to keep our security measures robust and up-to-date.
  • Access to the data is strictly limited to authorized research personnel and is governed by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • In the event of a data breach, affected participants will be notified promptly through a message on the Qualtrics study, and all necessary steps will be taken to minimize any potential impact.

5. Data Retention

The retention of collected data is managed as follows:

  • Data is retained for a period of three years post-study completion to allow for thorough analysis and publication of research findings.
  • After this period, all personal data will be either securely destroyed or anonymized, ensuring that individual participants cannot be identified from the retained information.
  • The decision to anonymize or destroy the data is contingent on its relevance to ongoing or future research, with a strong preference for anonymization to facilitate longitudinal studies.

6. IP Address and Session Tracking

While we do not actively collect IP addresses for profiling purposes, our systems automatically record session information as part of maintaining the integrity of the survey and its responses. This includes:

  • Date and time of access to the survey.
  • Duration of the session.
  • Any re-access patterns that might indicate repeated participation or technical issues.
  • This information is crucial in preventing fraudulent entries and ensuring the reliability of our survey results.

7. Participant Consent

Your participation in the survey is contingent upon your consent, which is considered granted upon your survey engagement. This consent includes agreement to:

  • The collection and analysis of your survey responses and interaction data.
  • The use of this data in accordance with this privacy policy.
  • We assure you that your participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw your consent at any time without any adverse consequences.
  • You understand that the real goal of this study is to track whether you read this privacy policy, so please refrain from sharing this with others as this might compromise study validity.

8. Your Rights

As a participant, you are entitled to certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: If you find that any data we hold is incorrect, you have the right to have it corrected.
  • Deletion: Under certain conditions, you may request the deletion of your data from our records.
  • These rights can be exercised through the contact details provided in section 10.

Please note your participant ID in the link you're currently viewing if you plan on using these rights, as otherwise you can not be identified anymore. Storing this link should be sufficient.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy may undergo amendments:

  • Any changes will be communicated through our study platform. Check the link for updates.
  • We encourage periodic review of this policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
  • Continued participation in the study after such changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
